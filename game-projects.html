Stellar Defense

Project Overview

A tower defense game set in deep space where players defend their space station against waves of alien invaders. Strategic placement of defensive turrets and resource management are key to survival.

Key Features

  • Campaign mode with branching storylines
  • Endless survival mode with competitive leaderboards
  • 15+ unique tower types with upgrade paths
  • Strategic resource management system
  • Dynamic enemy AI with varied attack patterns

Gameplay

Players must balance offensive and defensive strategies while managing limited resources. Each tower type has strengths and weaknesses against different enemy types, requiring tactical thinking.

Technologies Used

Unity Engine, AI Pathfinding, Particle Systems, Custom Tower System
