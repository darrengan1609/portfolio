ryo's cozy nook - serenity in luminescence

Whenever I just want to relax and unwind, I boot up my Minecraft and start building away, its one of my favorite comfort games. I take inspiration from various tutorials and personify it with my own elements.

This particular build is heavily cottage core inspired, with warm lighting and cozy aesthetics to create my chill and serene village.
All the color palette, interior, texturing and teraforming was all me, 100% my design pallete! I really enjoyed working on this project and I hope you all like it too!

Version: Minecraft 1.21.10
Shader: BSL
Mods: Iris, Fabric, Sodium
Primary Resource Pack: jehkoba's villagers, nautilus 3D, better dogs, lily's revamped animals, bees reimagined
