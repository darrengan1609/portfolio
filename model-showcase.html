3D MODELLING SHOWCASE

Goat Demon

  • Software: Blender, Substance Painter
  • Polycount: 182k tris
  • Inspiration: Resident Evil 4 Cult
  • Tags: goat, demon, creature, horror

Goat demon was created as the main antagonist for one of the game projects, My Pretty Neighbour. The demon features a humanoid body with a goat skull head, twisted horns growing from its eye sockets, dressed in tattered cult robe. It was designed to embody cult horror and psychological unease when chasing players around the map.

Barbarian Warrior

  • Software: ZBrush, Blender, 3DsMax, Substance Painter
  • Polycount: 185.6k tris
  • Inspiration: God of War, MLBB Phoveus
  • Tags: warrior, character, barbarian, fantasy

Burken of the Wild was created as the harbringer barbarian as part of sculpting project, Fantasticles. The character features rugged and worn-out armour, adorned with feathers and leather accents, wielding a massive bone sytche. The design draws inspiration from historical barbarian warriors and fantasy art, aiming to capture the essence of a fierce and untamed fighter.
